### Supervised vs. Unsupervised Learning Systems

**Key Finding:** Research distinguishes between problematic **uncontrolled self-reinforcement** and beneficial **supervised evolutionary systems**:
- **Model collapse occurs in unsupervised systems** training on their own outputs

- **Human-guided selection** enables beneficial evolution without collapse

**Research Evidence:**
- **Rice University (2024)**: Model collapse in 3-5 generations for unsupervised systems
- **Constitutional AI research (2024)**: External principle-based validation prevents bias amplification
- **Bayesian healthcare AI (2024)**: Proper uncertainty quantification enables safe adaptation

**Source:** ICLR 2024, Anthropic Constitutional AI Research (2024)

**Validation:** TPN-Prime's human clinical oversight and external validation mechanisms (Workflow 4) implement exactly the safeguards that research shows prevent model collapse while enabling beneficial adaptation.
